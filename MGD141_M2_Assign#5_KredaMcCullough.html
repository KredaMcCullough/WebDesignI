ANGEL SOUL THERAPY TYPE SPECIFICATION

Developer: Kreda McCullough

The following fonts will be used on the Angel Soul Therapy website:

  • Playfair Display
  • Open Sans

Playfair Display uses:

h1 as Playfair Display Black on the home page as the title of the website

h2 in bold in the slideshow showing the name of each page

h1 on the following pages:

  • About
  • Connecting With The Angels
  • Meet The Angels (including the Archangel Pages)
  • Angel Numbers
  • Blog

h2 on the following pages:

  • Archangel Name
  • Angel Numbers (Query)
  • Blog (Blog Entry Header)
  • Blog Entry Header (separate page)

Open Sans uses:

Navigation Bar

p in the slideshow on the home page as a small description under the name of each page

p copyright information in the footer.

h3 on Connecting With The Angels page

p as text information on the following pages:

  • About
  • Connecting With The Angel
  • Meet The Angels (including Archangel Pages)
  • Angel Numbers
  • Affirmations
  • Blog (including Blog Pages)
